## Notes

- 戦時中 teams used 軍 (gun/army) suffix
- EGL merged into KRW (黒鷲軍) in 1940
- KGR became SKR (松竹ロビンス) in 1947
- KSS (金星) and DES (大映) merged in 1957 → eventually became Chiba Lotte
- NSC and NNP merged in 1951 → became NSL (西鉄ライオンズ)
